# Water Efficiency

- Pair low-flow fixtures with efficient hot-water routing so both water use and energy demand drop together.
- Use rainwater capture for landscape or non-potable uses when local regulations and budget allow.
- Favor permeable paving, drought-tolerant planting, and hydrozoned landscaping to reduce runoff and irrigation demand.
- Keep plumbing cores compact to reduce pipe runs, heat loss, and installation cost.

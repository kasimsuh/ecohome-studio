# Climate Resilience

- Match the envelope, roof form, drainage, and service placement to the local climate risk profile from the start.
- In cold climates, prioritize airtight detailing, insulation continuity, and heat-recovery ventilation.
- In flood-prone areas, protect critical systems, elevate vulnerable zones, and design for fast recovery after water events.
- In tropical and humid climates, use breathable assemblies, moisture management, and shaded ventilation strategies.
